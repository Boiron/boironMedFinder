Warnings & Information

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes, mucous membranes, wounds, damaged, or irritated skin. Do not use if you are allergic to Arnica montana or to any of this product's inactive ingredients. When using this product use only as directed do not tightly wrap or bandage the treated area do not apply heat or ice to the treated area immediately before or after use. Stop use and ask a doctor if condition persists for more than 3 days or worsens. Keep out of reach of children. If swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away.

Arnicare Roll-on

Temporarily relieves muscle pain and stiffness due to minor injuries, overexertion, and falls. Reduces pain, swelling, and discoloration from bruises.
