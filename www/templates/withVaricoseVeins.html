Warnings & Information

Bruising increases with age: the skin is thinner, and the capillaries are more fragile. Bruising can also be caused by medications or some supplements. See a doctor if bruising develops for no apparent reason, if abnormal bleeding (from the nose or gums, or in the intestine) occurs, or if you recently started taking a new medication or supplement.
