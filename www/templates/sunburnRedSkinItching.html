Dosage:

For minor burns, immediately run cool water on the burn for several minutes and dry. For cuts, scrapes and chafing, first cleanse the area with mild soap, rinse and dry. Do not use if you are allergic to Calendula officinalis or to this product's inactive ingredients. Ask a doctor before use in case of deep or puncture wounds, animal bites or serious burns.
